Comprehensive Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Project Title: VibeFit AI Vision: A minimalist, high-utility fitness companion that removes the friction of manual data entry through "Photo-First" logging.

1. User Interface & Experience (UI/UX)
Design System: * Primary Background: Creme (#FFFDD0 or #F5F5DC).

Containers/Cards: Pure White (#FFFFFF) with soft rounded corners (24px) and subtle shadows.

Typography: Playfair Display (Headings) and Inter (Body).

Navigation: A bottom navigation bar for mobile-first use (Dashboard, Log, Analytics, Profile).

2. Feature Breakdown
A. The "Photo Log" Hub
Scale Scanner: User takes a photo of their digital/analog scale. The app must extract the numerical value and auto-populate the weight field.

Lens Integration: A "Scan" button that triggers the camera.

Scenario A (Food): Identifies the dish (e.g., "Chicken Biryani") and estimates calories/macros.

Scenario B (Gym): Identifies equipment (e.g., "Lat Pulldown Machine") and suggests 3 variations of exercises.

B. The Workout Engine
Input Fields: A repeater-style form where a user adds an exercise.

Exercise Name (Autocomplete from a library).

Weight (in kg/lbs).

Reps (Count).

Sets (Count).

History: Ability to "Copy last workout" to save time.

C. Medication & Water Tracker
Daily Checklist: Simple toggle buttons for "Morning Meds," "Pre-workout," etc.

Visual Indicator: A water droplet icon that fills up as the user logs intake.

D. The AI Score & Insights
Daily Score (0-100): A circular progress gauge on the home screen.

Reasoning: A small "AI Coach" text box explaining why the score is X (e.g., "Your score is 85/100 because you hit your protein goal, but your sleep was inconsistent.").
